## About Me

### My Journey

Hello, and welcome to VictorVibes! I'm Victor, and this is my story.

Born in the city of Guaratingueta, SP, Brazil, in 2002, I spent my early childhood immersed in the rich culture of Brazil. At the age of nine, my life took a turn as my family and I went on a new adventure, moving to Tennessee, USA. This move opened up a world of opportunities and experiences that shaped my path forward.

After completing middle school in Tennessee, my family decided to move back to Brazil. However, my heart was set on the opportunities and lifestyle that the US had to offer. With the huge support of my family, I made the bold decision to move back to Tennessee at the age of 15, where I became emancipated and took charge of my own journey.

I completed my high school education at Cleveland High School in 2020, filled with determination and dreams for the future. Eager to go deeper into the world of technology, I moved to Cookeville to pursue a degree in computer science at Tennessee Technological University. Now, as a recent graduate, I am proud to have maintained a 4.0 GPA throughout my academic journey.

### Achievements and Passions

My journey has been filled with milestones and achievements that have fueled my growth:

- Becoming emancipated at the age of 16, a step that marked my independence and determination.
- Buying my first new car at 17, a moment of pride and freedom.
- Purchasing my own house at 18, a dream turned reality.
- Maintaining a 4.0 GPA throughout my college years.

Beyond academics and milestones, my life is enriched with hobbies and passions that bring joy and balance:

- **Motorcycling**: Riding my Ninja 400, feeling the wind and the sense of freedom.
- **Personal Finance**: Diving into the world of stock markets and investments, always looking for smart ways to grow my wealth.
- **Gun Range**: Embracing the focus and precision required at the gun range.
- **Traveling**: Exploring new places and cultures, from Canada to Mexico, the US, Brazil, and more.
- **Music**: Allowing my mood to match my surrounding and current task.
- **Gym**: Recently embracing the world of fitness and finding strength in the challenge.

### Looking Ahead

As I navigate my carrer and look ahead to the future, my aspirations are clear. I aim to secure a stable corporate job, while also exploring the vast world outside of work. From continuing my journey in investments to potentially pioneering a software company that seamlessly integrates the finance world with everyday usability, my dreams are big, and my journey is just beginning.

At VictorVibes, I invite you to join me on this journey, to explore the intersections of technology, personal growth, and the adventures that life has to offer. Let’s vibe with the future together!
